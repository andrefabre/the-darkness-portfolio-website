About

I have acquired and sharpened my expertise in cybersecurity risk assessment, compliance frameworks, and business security operations. My background in interpreting and applying the Australian Signals Directorate's Essential Eight guidelines enables me to bridge the gap between complex technical requirements and practical business implementation.

My approach focuses on building resilient security postures through rigorous assessment and practical policy development. I specialize in conducting comprehensive security risk assessments that analyze not just technical vulnerabilities, but also business processes and human factors that represent the greatest threats to organizations.

What sets me apart is my ability to communicate complex cybersecurity concepts to diverse stakeholders - from executive leadership to end-users - ensuring everyone understands their role in maintaining a secure environment. Whether managing a security incident or proactively building defensive systems, my goal is to deliver clear, actionable guidance that protects your organization while supporting business growth.
